About Krishan Attire

Krishan Attire is a humble offering of love and devotion to Thakur Ji. Every dress is crafted with care, beauty, and spiritual intention. From hand embroidery to vibrant colors, our mission is to bring joy to your pooja rituals.

Founded by Divya and Jyotsana, this project brings together devotion and design to serve those who love adorning Thakur Ji with grace.
